About Keyword Alignment

Keyword Alignment compares the words in the search term with the words in the matched keyword. It is a directional review signal only. It does not understand intent, synonyms, close variants, landing-page relevance, or conversion quality, and it does not affect flagged spend unless the term already fails a KPI rule.
